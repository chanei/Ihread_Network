About Us

Ihread Network was founded in September, 2016 but opened doors in August, 2017 during the growth of social media, and began by selling social media and video services. In less than a year, we were working with Fortune 500 brands in order to grow our agency. We have expanded our service offerings and became a full-service digital agency working with small, medium and large companies. From SEO services to paid advertising to front end and back end website and app development, we offer the full gamut. We’ve grown our business through digital marketing and we felt there was no better selling point to prospective clients than showing them what we’ve done for ourselves..
