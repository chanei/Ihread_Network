About Ihread Network

We have expanded our service offerings and became a full-service digital agency working with small, medium and large companies.
And we’re just getting started.

WHO IS IHREAD NETWORK?

Ihread Network is a full-service digital agency located in Kansanga, Kampala. Our motto is simple. We want to help your business generate more leads and sales through clever digital campaigns. Whether you need a new website to receive more conversions from visitors or want to improve your ranking on Google, we have a team of designers, developers and digital marketers that can make it happen. Even though we help clients both locally and nationally, our DNA is rooted in Uganda. This is beneficial for clients wanting to improve their local presence, or wanting to talk about the best new restaurant in town!

WHY CHOOSE A DIGITAL AGENCY?

The personalized strategy and direction of your digital campaign makes a big difference when it comes to generating results. Imagine writing a great book that nobody buys and therefore nobody reads. Your business is your book and we don’t want it to sit on the shelf collecting dust. We want everyone to know your story, so you can reap the benefits. We’re here to help you tell your story to the world. You can spend a lot of time and effort on your marketing plan, but if you aren’t focusing on the right channels or you don’t have a website that’s superior to your competitors, you could be wasting your time and money.

WHAT DIFFERENTIATES IHREAD NETWORK?

We love the business world. We’re going to learn about your company and reverse engineer your brand to better understand what makes customers or clients utilize your service or products. Then, we’ll come up with a digital strategy to justify the return on investment from your marketing efforts. We have designers and developers to make sure everything looks beautiful and functions flawlessly. Our main focus is driving more business for your business and every step we take has that goal in mind.

SMOOTH SAILING DIGITAL MARKETING

If we build your business a new site and your e-commerce conversion rate drastically increases, you can expect an email or text from someone on our team. The same goes for moving onto the first page of Google. We’re going to geek out about this move that can make a big difference towards your bottom line. We don’t just play defense. We are a proactive group of digital enthusiasts that will always bring new ideas to the table based off of the trends we see in the online world. If you work with Ihread Network, your experience will be smooth sailing, and you’ll certainly learn a thing or two along the way as well.
